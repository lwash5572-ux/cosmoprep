Hair Care · Question 3 of 50

What chemical process occurs when hydrogen peroxide is added to hair color?

○A. Reduction
✓B. Oxidation
○C. Hydration
○D. Saturation
💡

Bundle includes answer explanations like this:

Correct Answer: B — Oxidation. When hydrogen peroxide (the developer) is mixed with hair color, it triggers an oxidation reaction. This opens the hair cuticle and allows the color molecules to penetrate the cortex, where they develop and bond to create the final color result.
